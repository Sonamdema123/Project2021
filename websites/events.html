Events

These is the event which was happened in the Gyalpozhing college of information technology after 2019 first year students joined in the college. Some important events are given below and those who want to learn more about college events or want to view more images than kindly visit the link which is shared in bottom of events.




1 November 2019

2019 Foundation day held on 1st of Nov. in the presence of His Eminence Lhalung Sungtrul Rinpoche.

11 November 2019

On this auspicious and joyous occasion of the 64th Birth Anniversary of the Fourth Druk Gyalpo, Jigme Singye Wangchuck, Gyalpozhing College of Information Technology joins the nation in offering profound prayers for His long life and good health.

Happy Birthday Your Majesty!

20 February 2020

On this most joyous occasion of His Majesty's 40th Birthday, the family of Gyalpozhing College of Information Technology,Mongar would like to join the nation in offering our deepest respect, heartfelt felicitation and genuine gratitude to His Majesty The Druk Gyalpo.

May Your Majesty live a happy, healthy, and long life!


22 Febraury 2020

On behalf of Gyalpoizhing Hospital family, would like to extend our great appreciation and big thanks to all the students of GCIT, (tarayana Club) Gyalpoizhing Mongar, for your contribution in mass cleaning and wet washing of our hospital on 22:02:2020.

27 Febraury 2020

We are delivering a three-day media literacy training at Gyalpozhing College of Information Technology. GCIT is the newest college in the country. The training is part of a three-tier media literacy programme supported by EU/Helvetas.
