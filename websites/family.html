MY VILLAGE


About Village

Our village is known as Kheri nestled on the top of the mountain. It is surrounded by Lush environment where prevails peace and calm nature accompanied by the inhabits of numerous animals and birds. Our village is scattered settlement and people earn the livelihood through domestication of animals and farming.

It is two hours walk from the Dzongkhag headquater and located under Shumar geog under PemaGatshel Dzongkhag. Now, village was little bit developed road is connected and no more tension to carry load in back. when I was class four or five there are few houses only but now i can see more houeses are built and atmosphere is good while compare to before.
