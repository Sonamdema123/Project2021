Experience in My Life

Hand Made Chips

I also learnt making potato chips.Potato chips is one of the most common snacks which is a favorite for all. The potatoes chips are hand picked and flavored with pepper or red chilli powder.Ingredients include such Pepper, salt, and chili power.

steps to making potato chips:

  • Wash and peel of the potato.
  • Slicing the potato and drop into the water.
  • Wash the potato slice 3 or 4 times until water run out clean.
  • After 10-15 min remove the chips from water and leave to dry it.
  • Drop the batch of potato slice in the oil, until they turn crisp.
  • Add ingredients and than packing for sale.
