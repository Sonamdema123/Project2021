My journey of education




When I was age of six I joined school at Shali Primary school under Pemagatshel dzongkhag. I studied till grade three at there and year of 2010 when i was in grade four I moved to Shumar lower secondary school. I was dayscholar and we need to carry pack lunch every day because we have to walk for almost two hours from school and there was no such bus services.



when I was at lower school I had participate in culture and some volunteer work but i never participated in sport session. On 2014 I completed class eight from shumar LSS and I got admission at Nangkor HSS.

Those who either want to visit Nangkor facebook page or want to know more about school, you can click on button to visit the Nangkor page.



My High School

There are two high school at Pemagatshel Nangkor HSS and Ngalam CS.Those who qualify from class ten from Yurung CS, Pemagatshel MSS they are joining to Nangkor. I got admission at Nangkor school as a border. There are only two section in class nine and ten and I was section B and I took agriculture subject. When I was grade ten I recieved best border girl award and I also qualified from class ten so I acheived my dream and parents dream.


When I was at high school my dream is to become an engineer and I really want to experience college life and then I took pure maths in class Eleven. My best subject is maths and i enjoy learning maths and other subject also. All teachers are supportive, concern, guide in every moment and they made our life in better place. I studied at Nangkor for four year and I learned so many lesson from there. I completed my high school at 2018 with joy and happiness with wonderful teachers and friend
